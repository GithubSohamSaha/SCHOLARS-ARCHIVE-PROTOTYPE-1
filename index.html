Welcome to Scholar's Archive

This demo lets different types of users (Users, Librarians, Stock Managers, Accountants) explore how a digital library system works.

Use the navigation above to log in, sign up, or read the Support page for guidance.

Quick Start

  • New here? Click Sign Up and create a User account.
  • Already have an account? Click Login.
  • Need help deciding your role? Visit the Support page.

Support & FAQs

Choose the question that matches what you want to do. Each answer can take you directly to the right place in the app.

1. I am a regular user who wants to borrow books. What should I do?

If you only need to search and borrow books, use the User role.

  1. Click the button below to go to the login screen with the User role pre-selected.
  2. Log in with your username and password, or sign up if you are new.
  3. After login, use Search Books, My Books, and Pay Fines from your dashboard.

2. I manage the library catalogue and users. Which role should I use?

Use the Librarian role to view all books, all users, fines, and stock overview, and to issue books.

3. I take care of stock and new book orders.

Use the Stock Manager role to add books and raise order requests.

4. I handle fines and payments.

Use the Accountant role to see fine reports, acknowledgements, issue receipts, and create new fines.

5. How do I sign up as a new user?

  1. Click the Sign Up link in the top navigation.
  2. Fill in your Name, Username, Email, Password, and choose a Role.
  3. Submit the form, then use Login with the same username, password, and role.
